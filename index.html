The Problem

Warehouses Face Critical Challenges

High Labor Costs

Manual picking is labor-intensive, increasingly expensive due to rising wages, and faces a growing shortage of available workers.

Space Inefficiency

Limited vertical reach by human pickers restricts storage density, forcing larger, costlier warehouses.

Scaling Difficulties

Hard to scale due to workforce hiring, training, and management challenges.

Big Market Gap

No cost-effective and simple automation solution for small and medium sized warehouses.

Our solution: Cut labor costs by 5x, double storage density, enable effortless scaling—while prioritizing human dignity.
